Who We Are ?

Broke to Billionaire (BTB) is a youth-led creative and innovation venture founded by 3 college students in India.

Born from the belief that greatness can start from nothing, BTB bridges the gap between learning and doing — empowering young people to transform ideas into impactful realities.

Our Core Belief

“We’re not waiting for opportunities — we’re creating them.”

BTB is where passion meets execution. We merge creativity, storytelling, and technology to shape the leaders of tomorrow.

Our Mission

To empower the next generation of creators, coders, and dreamers with the mindset, skills, and resources to turn ordinary beginnings into extraordinary achievements.

Our Vision

To become India’s leading youth innovation ecosystem — connecting education, entrepreneurship, and digital creativity into a single growth movement.
